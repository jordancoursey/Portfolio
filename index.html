Party Planner

Party Planning App - Android Mobile (Java)close

An application used to coordinate the logistics of planning a party. Relevant information is distributed only to necessary individuals for a streamlined planning process. Hosts are able to make lists that allow party-goers to bring items requested by the host in a transparent environment. Polling capabilities allow for hosts to make decisions about the party that appeal to the most party-goers. The app utilizes the Google Maps API to show the location of the party. Users can also display their own location to others invited to the party as an option to enhance ride-sharing possibilities among party-goers.
